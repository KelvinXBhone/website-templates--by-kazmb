FAQs About Standing Desk Health Benefits

Standing Desk Benefits

Reduced Sedentary Time: Using a standing desk can significantly reduce the time spent sitting, which helps engage muscles, improve mobility, and strengthen joints.

Caloric Burn: While standing burns slightly more calories than sitting (about 88 calories per hour vs. 80 calories), not a significant difference. Walking, however, burns 210 calories per hour1. So, consider short walks during breaks for better results.

Blood Sugar Regulation: Some studies suggest that standing after meals may help normalize blood sugar levels faster.

Posture Improvement: Standing encourages better posture and reduces the risk of back pain.
